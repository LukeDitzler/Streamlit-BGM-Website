NFL Analytical Dataset Schema

1. Core Player Stats (Regular Season)

player_id: Unique GSIS identifier (NFL-standard).
player_display_name: Playerâ€™s full name.
position: Primary position (QB, RB, WR, TE).
season: NFL season year (e.g., 2024).
recent_team: The team the player was on at the end of the season.
games: Total regular-season games played.
completions / attempts: Passing completions and pass attempts.
passing_yards / passing_tds / passing_interceptions: Standard passing production.
sack_fumbles: Fumbles lost or occurring during a sack.
passing_epa: Total Expected Points Added on passing plays.
carries / rushing_yards / rushing_tds: Standard rushing production.
rushing_epa: Total Expected Points Added on rushing plays.
receptions / targets: Number of catches and number of times targeted.
receiving_yards / receiving_tds: Standard receiving production.
receiving_air_yards: Depth of targets in yards (measured from line of scrimmage).
receiving_epa: Total Expected Points Added on receiving plays.
target_share: Percentage of team targets directed to this player.
fantasy_points: Total standard fantasy points.
fantasy_points_half_ppr: Fantasy points calculated with 0.5 points per reception.
fantasy_points_per_game: Half-PPR points divided by games played.

2. Team-Level Totals (for Market Share Calcs)

team_carries / team_rushing_yards / team_rushing_tds: Total team rushing volume.
team_receptions / team_receiving_yards / team_receiving_tds: Total team receiving volume.

3. PFR Advanced Passing Metrics

pfr_id: Pro Football Reference unique identifier.
team: Team abbreviation used in PFR datasets.
drop_pct: Percentage of passes dropped by receivers.
bad_throw_pct: Percentage of throws that were not on target (excluding spikes/throwaways).
on_tgt_pct: Percentage of throws that were on target.
pocket_time: Average time (seconds) spent in the pocket between snap and throw/pressure.
pressure_pct: Percentage of dropbacks where the QB was pressured.
times_blitzed: Number of times the opposing defense sent a blitz.
intended_air_yards_per_pass_attempt: Average depth of all passes (IAY/PA).
completed_air_yards_per_pass_attempt: Average depth of completed passes only.
rpo_rush_att / rpo_rush_yards: Volume and production from RPO-designed runs.
scrambles / scramble_yards_per_attempt: Frequency and efficiency of non-designed QB runs.

4. PFR Advanced Rushing (Prefixed: rush_)

rush_ybc_att: Yards before contact per rushing attempt.
rush_yac_att: Yards after contact per rushing attempt.
rush_brk_tkl: Total number of tackles broken while rushing.
rush_att_br: Attempts per broken tackle (efficiency metric).
rush_x1d: Rushing attempts resulting in a first down.
rush_loaded: Number of carries against a "loaded box" (8+ defenders).

5. PFR Advanced Receiving (Prefixed: rec_)

rec_adot: Average depth of target.
rec_yac_r: Yards after catch per reception.
rec_ybc_r: Yards before catch (air yards) per reception.
rec_brk_tkl: Number of tackles broken after a catch.
rec_rec_br: Receptions per broken tackle.
rec_x1d: Receptions resulting in a first down.
rec_drop_percent: Percentage of targets dropped by the player.

6. FTN Charting Data (Flags)

is_catchable_ball: Boolean/Flag for whether the target was physically catchable.
is_contested_ball: Boolean/Flag for whether a defender was in range to contest.
is_drop: Boolean/Flag confirming a dropped pass.
is_interception_worthy: Flag for QB throws that "should" have been intercepted.

7. Roster, Draft, and Contract Data

draft_round / draft_pick_num: Round and overall pick in the NFL Draft.
draft_team: The team that originally drafted the player.
draft_age: Player's age on the day they were drafted.
birth_date: Player's date of birth.
years_exp: Number of years of NFL experience.
apy: Average Per Year contract value.
contract_years_remaining: Number of seasons left on current deal.
is_contract_year: Boolean/Flag indicating if the player is in the final year of their deal.
season_age: Player's age during the specific season (calculated).
